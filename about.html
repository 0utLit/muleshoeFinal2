Meet The Owner

Cliff Dvorak has been the owner of Muleshoe Antiques for 10 years. In 2000, he was looking at small towns to settle down with his wife after college, when he found Victor. He became inspired by the amazing historical architecture, the rich history, and the friendly quiet people. He could also fufill his passions for treasure hunting. He has been all over the Cripple Creek district searching for coins, jewlery, bottles, rusty junk, and much more, for the 24 years he has lived here. He also has had a field day in colorado going to thrift stores, yard and estate sales, trading, buying and selling antiques. At a certain point, there was too much stuff, so he decided to open up an Antique store across the street in order to get rid of some of it.
